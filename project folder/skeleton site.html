Beginner tips and tricks

Fishing is an easy sport for anyone to get into. It is also very easy to start to learn. To start all that is needed is
1. Fishing rod
2. Lures/Bait
With these two tools, you can catch hundreds of fish.

One of the easiest and more fun fish to catch is the bluegill. This fish goes by a couple names such as panfish and pumpkin seed fish. They can be found all over the world in fresh water. All that is needed to catch bluegill is
1. Bait
a. Bread, meat, cheese, and worms
2. Hook
a. Small hook is best due to these fish getting no larger than 1lbs
